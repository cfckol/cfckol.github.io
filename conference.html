Who We Are

Calcutta Foodies Club was founded on 12th August, 2014 by Dr. Agniva Chakraborty. We, Calcutta Foodies Club, are a non-profit organization based in Kolkata, India. We are primarily active in Facebook where member count is about 100,000. Such a huge number of people from different backgrounds are bonded only by their love for food and related activities. From simple food lover to cooking enthusiast to professional chef and restaurant owner, everyone uses this common platform to exchange information, experience and opinion. Besides our virtual activities, we frequently organize meets and events to promote the offline interaction between members. We take immense interest in various social welfare activities since inception. Our members are always eager to help the underprivileged people of our society. We have organized several events to reach out and support them. We also facilitate advocacy initiatives and awareness generation programmes.
