Our commitment to build better society

Love for food, the primary cause of Calcutta Foodies Club, is not just limited to own gastronomical ecstasy. Seeing others to have similar experience makes us very happy as well. Although our initial introduction is “we eat we drink we share on CFC”, it is way more than that. It's also “Eat and Let Eat”, and most importantly it's also 'Let's Feed'. And that is what we did repeatedly since inception. Our members also love to extend helping hand for the underprivileged community. They eagerly participate and voluntarily give various gifts of their own in all our social activity events. And this is what makes us special.
